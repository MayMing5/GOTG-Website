THIS IS OUR STORY

The journey began in 2006 while attending culinary school in Florida. Before finishing school, I fell upon hardships and had to return to North Carolina. I initially started out perfecting my craft with BBQ/Soul food. We started selling BBQ and ribs while researching ways to start a trailer. My plan was to be the BBQ king.

I joined many cook offs and even became a certified judge. We found that simplifying the menu was the fastest and best option to get us up and running. So, while BBQ was my passion, making phillies and salads would be where we started with the menu.

While working to perfect my grilling skills, from 2007-2019 I worked at various restaurants. The restaurants ranged from substations to high end restaurants and finally my last official place of employment when COVID struck, a corporate/ government cafeteria.
